Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

This is a personal dashboard app used to view my own health and fitness data, including data from connected services such as WHOOP.

What data is accessed

  • Recovery, sleep, strain, and biometric data (HRV, resting heart rate, skin temperature, blood oxygen, respiratory rate) from the WHOOP API.

How data is used

  • Data is displayed only within this dashboard, for personal use.
  • Data is not sold, shared, or used for advertising.
  • Data is not shared with any third party.

Where data is stored

  • Access tokens and fetched data are stored locally in the browser (localStorage) and/or in a private database used solely by this app.
  • No data is stored on third-party servers beyond what is required to operate this app.

Revoking access

You can disconnect WHOOP at any time from within the dashboard, or by revoking access directly from your WHOOP developer/account settings.

Questions about this policy can be sent to leonsalita@gmail.com.
